Can't Log In?
You have reached this screen because you are affiliated with Indiana University, but your account has one of the following issues:
  • You do not appear to be a student, faculty or staff member on the Indianapolis campus, OR
  • You are attempting to access a Law School resource restricted to Law School affiliates only.
Library electronic subscription resources (e-books, e-journals, databases) are purchased for a particular campus or school and license agreements allow only users affiliated with that campus or school to use them.
  • If you are an IUPUI campus student, faculty, or staff member, or you are a Law School affiliate, contact the UITS Support Center to have a consultant check your account.
  • If you are a student or faculty member at a different IU campus and taking or teaching a class at IUPUI, please read the policy, and if it applies, complete the form at the bottom of that page.
  • If you are a faculty member who needs access to IUPUI University Library electronic resources for research, email for assistance.
  • If you are a faculty member who needs access to Robert H. McKinney Law School (Indianapolis) electronic resources for research, chat with the Law School Library for assistance.
  • If you are a student, faculty or staff member from a campus outside IUPUI and do not fall into one of these groups, please contact the library at your campus for assistance.
